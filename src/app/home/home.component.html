Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

Does Eco-friendly homes costs less?
There is no such compulsion that a eco home should cost less. It mainly depends on material you choose and its availability.
Is Mud contruction reliable?
Ofcourse, mud contructions have been practised for thousands of years and are proven to be stronger as it ages.
Can we acheive strength without cement?
Mud and lime combination can eliminate the absolute usage of cement in residential buildings.
Why should we eliminate steel in homes?
Because steel resist natural forces and does not co-exist,so it wont last long.
What is breathable homes?
Like all living beings homes should breathe for its own life which can be acheived through proper usage of materials.
Can bamboo replace steel?
Studies suggests that bamboo can be a very good alternative for steel and history also has proof for using bamboo as dominant building material.
How we make low cost homes?
Best way is to choose what you have in your land or your surrounding and go for it. Dont forget to involve an expert for guidance.
Can we raise floors through filler slab?
Why not? Filler slab is not far behind conventional RCC roofing.
What is Carbon footprint of a building?
Every thing in this world emits carbon and for a better home ,we should design in such a way that there is only minimal carbon emission.
Is lime better than cement?
Considering the reversible nature of lime,it is way ahead of cement and hence its been used for centuries in various civilizations.
